About Christian Liew

Hello, my name is Christian Liew, and at the age of 19, I have already experienced a rather unique childhood that has greatly influenced the person I am today. Allow me to share a glimpse of my journey and shed light on what inspired me to embark on the path of acquiring the intricate skill of coding.

Born in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, I had the opportunity to live in different countries throughout my childhood. Alongside my family, I spent significant periods in Hong Kong, Japan, and Malaysia. This multicultural exposure broadened my horizons and cultivated my adaptability and open-mindedness.

At the age of 5, I discovered my love for tennis. The sport captured my heart, and I began training diligently to pursue my dream of becoming a professional tennis player. As I progressed, the demands of tournaments and rigorous training schedules started to clash with my academic commitments. Recognizing the need for flexibility, I made the decision to switch to homeschooling in grade 9, allowing me to dedicate more time to honing my skills and participating in international tennis events.

Although I was homeschooled throughout my highschool career, I approached it with the same level of dedication I had for tennis. I undertook rigurous courses in the form of the IGCSE and A-level curricula. Maintaining good grades with intense tennis training and traveling became a challenging yet rewarding experience. Through this experience I was able to grow a lot as a person and it helped me develop strong self-discipline, and great time management skills .

Upon completing my high school education, I found myself with a gap between finishing in November and starting college in September. I was eager to utilize this time productively and explore new areas of interest. With an open mind and a thirst for knowledge, I turned to coding. As someone who was homeschooled and primarily focused on tennis, I hadn't fully discovered my academic passion. Coding presented an exciting opportunity to explore a field that combined logic, problem-solving, and creativity.

To embark on my coding journey, I initially enrolled in We Stride's full-stack development bootcamp. Unfortunately, the program's international division was discontinued shortly after I joined. However, it was through this program that I had the fortune of meeting Lea, a coding mentor who played a crucial role in my development. Recognizing our strong connection and her expertise, I asked Lea to continue mentoring me as I transitioned to Colt Steele's web development course on Udemy. Lea's guidance, patience, and industry insights have been invaluable, and our weekly meetings have provided a platform to address challenges and foster growth.

My experience with coding has been both exhilarating and demanding. For example, I anticipated picking up JavaScript quickly, but it proved to be more challenging than expected. However, I persevered, recognizing that learning to code requires time, dedication, and continuous effort. While juggling commitments such as participating in the South East Asian Games and completing an internship, progress has been slower than anticipated. Nevertheless, I remain committed to mastering the course material and continuing my coding journey as I embark on my college education at Emory University.

Looking ahead, I am considering pursuing a degree in computer science, allowing me to delve deeper into the fundamentals of coding. I am particularly interested in exploring Python and its applications in machine learning, including transformative technologies like ChatGPT. The prospect of contributing to the development of powerful AI that can improve lives excites me, and I'm eager to explore the possibilities within this field.

Learning to code has revealed the power and practicality of this skill. It has become evident that not many individuals possess coding knowledge, and this realization has reinforced my belief in the importance of acquiring these skills. I am determined to deliver a successful outcome, knowing that it will serve as a valuable addition to my resume.

My journey thus far has been marked by diverse experiences, intense dedication to tennis and education, and an unwavering commitment to self-improvement. Through homeschooling, I found the flexibility to pursue my passion for tennis while still nurturing my academic growth. Exploring coding has opened up a world of possibilities and ignited a desire to delve deeper into the realm of computer science. With each challenge, I continue to grow, adapt, and push the boundaries of my capabilities. I am excited to embrace new opportunities, expand my knowledge, and contribute to the exciting field of coding in pursuit of a future that combines technology and positive impact.
